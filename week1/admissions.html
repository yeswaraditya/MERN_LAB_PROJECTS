Admissions

The admissions to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs at PVPSIT are based on entrance examinations and qualifying marks. The application forms are available on the official website or can be obtained directly from the institute.

Admission Procedure:

Admission into UG and PG programmes of the Institution is based on State level Common Entrance Test conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Admission of the students into Undergraduate Engineering programme of the institution is based on the State Level Common Entrance Test i.e., EAMCET / ECET, conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Admission of the students into MBA course is based on the state level common Entrance Test, ICET, conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Admission of the students into M.Tech programme is based on PGECET conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh and All India GATE exam.

Eligibility:

The eligibility criteria for admission into UG & PG programmes are as per the norms approved by Government of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

Categories:

Category - A:

For both U.G and P.G Programs, 70% of the seats will be allocated based on the common entrance test i.e., EAMCET/ICET/PGECET conducted by Government of Andhra Pradesh and GATE Rank based for M.Tech. Admission.

Category - B:

30% of the seats are filled under Management / NRI category as per the norms / regulations prescribed by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education.

Lateral Entry Programmes:

10% of the actual intake and vacancy seats of Convener Quota in the previous year (if any) seats are filled under the Lateral Entry based on the common entrance test i.e., ECET conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
