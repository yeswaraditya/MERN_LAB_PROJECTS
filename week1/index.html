Brief Profile

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. The college is located in Kanuru, Vijayawada, and offers various engineering and management programs.

The college is autonomous, accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade, and placed in the NIRF Innovation stream. The highest pay package for placements is 44 lakhs.

Hallmarks

  • NAAC A+ grade accredited
  • All UG Programs are NBA accredited
  • ISO 9001-2015 certified
  • 100% ragging free environment
  • Approved by AICTE and permanently affiliated to JNTUK
  • Autonomous College Status since 2012
  • Curriculum aligned with current and future industrial requirements
  • Strong Training and Placement Cell
  • Entrepreneurship support for students
  • Active NSS and NCC wings
  • Technical/Professional Societies for learning beyond classroom
  • Cultural/Hobby Clubs for all-around development of students
