Academics

PVPSIT offers a diverse range of academic programs, ensuring that students receive the highest quality education. The curriculum is designed according to industry standards and is constantly updated to match emerging trends in technology.

Undergraduate Programs:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering (CE)
  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • B.Tech in Information Technology (IT)

Postgraduate Programs:

  • M.Tech in ECE, ME
  • MBA
