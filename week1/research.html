Research & Innovation

PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology fosters an innovative culture through its research and innovation initiatives. We provide opportunities for students and faculty to engage in meaningful research projects and collaborations with industry leaders.

Research Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Science
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Embedded Systems
  • Renewable Energy Technologies

Research Committees:

  • Research Advisory Committee
  • IPR Committee
  • Constitution of Ethics Committee
  • Apex Committee

Research Promotion Policy:

As per the vision and mission statement, the institute provides all required facilities and a promising environment to promote research activities in the departments. The following are the initiatives to promote research activities in the departments:

  • Encourage faculty to undertake research relevant to the societal needs and industry.
  • Provide modern research facilities such as computational/software/hardware tools required for research by faculty and students.
  • Promote Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs) with industries and institutions for enhancing research activities and sharing resources available.
  • Provide seed money to faculty for conducting research.
  • Include research topics/courses in the curriculum of UG/PG, such as Research Methodology, Term Paper, Field Projects, and Industrial Internships, to improve the research capabilities of students.
  • Provide necessary support for inter-departmental and intra-institutional collaboration required for fostering research activities.
  • Provide incentives to faculty for publishing research papers in indexed journals.

Objectives:

  • Create awareness of various opportunities in R&D.
  • Utilize infrastructural facilities and undertake research projects in emerging areas offered by agencies such as ISRO, DRDO, CSIR, DST, AICTE, UGC, etc.
  • Guide the faculty to get funds for conducting Seminars/Workshops/FDPs from various funding agencies.
  • Offer financial support for innovative research by faculty and students and for participating in National/International Conferences, Training programs, and Tech-Festivals organized by reputed institutes (IITs/NITs/Universities).

Research Promotion Policy:

The R&D Cell is run by a research committee to plan, execute, and monitor the research activity progress once each quarter of the year.

  • Encourage faculty to undertake research relevant to societal needs and industry.
  • Expect faculty to generate funds for supporting their research activities, publications in Scopus or SCI journals, and establish research units.
  • The main objective of the R&D Cell is to encourage faculty to undertake research relevant to societal needs and industry and promote and develop research capabilities in students.
