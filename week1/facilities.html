Library:

The library at PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology provides access to a wide range of books, journals, and online resources to support academic and research activities.

Central Library

The Central Library is one of the most important facilities at PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology, catering to the information and research needs of faculty and students. Originating in 1998, the library follows an Open Access System. All books are arranged according to the Dewey Decimal Classification 21st edition, making it easy for users to locate their desired books.

The library is fully automated with LIBSYS10 LMS (Library Management Software), supporting 20,000 unique records. It is web-centric and provides access to over 1.2 million records through an online catalog system (OPAC), available to 25 concurrent users at a time.
